About Me

I am a senior data engineer looking for my next adventure. With over 15 years of experience in data engineering and business intelligence, I have developed a strong foundation in managing data tools and leading analytics teams. My expertise in SQL, data visualization, and project management makes me an ideal candidate for this role.

In my previous role at Forcepoint LLC, I managed a team of data engineers and served as a technical lead for our critical financial reporting datasets. I have extensive experience with SQL, including writing and optimizing complex queries, which is critical for the Senior Analyst role. I have also automated data flows and create data models using SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS) and SQL Server Analysis Services (SSAS). I was also a leading proponent for adopting a Tableau as our visualization tool for analytic reporting and served as the administrator.

At Vaya Health, I have been leading my team through a complicated transition to whole person health. With this transition my team and I had to face many new challenges like incorporating pharmacy and medical data from third party vendors into our data warehouse as well as assigning members to providers using an automated best fit process.

Outside managing my team at Vaya, I have also worked on honing my python programming skills while doing good. As the VP of Administration for Austin Junior Forum, I was able to write a program to read and calculate total volunteer hours for each member and send an automated email with their individual information.

I am committed to fostering an inclusive team culture and mentoring new data engineers aligns with Home Health AI values. I am eager to contribute my skills and experience to help Home Health AI drive strategic decision-making and support the success of small businesses.
